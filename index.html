Module 1: Misinformation vs Disinformation

Misinformation is false or inaccurate information shared without intent to deceive.

Disinformation is false information shared with the intent to mislead.

Module 4: Evaluate Credibility

Use this checklist to evaluate any post:

  • Who: Is the author credible?
  • What: Is the source trustworthy?
  • When: Is the information current?
  • Where: Are facts and sources cited?
  • Why: Is the intent informative or misleading?
