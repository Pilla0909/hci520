Module 4: Evaluate Credibility

Use this checklist to evaluate any post:

  • Who: Is the author credible?
  • What: Is the source reliable?
  • When: Is it current?
  • Where: Are facts and sources cited?
  • Why: Was it created to inform or mislead?
