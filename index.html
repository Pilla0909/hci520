Module 4: Evaluate Credibility

Use this checklist to evaluate online content before sharing:

  • Who: Is the author credible?
  • What: Is the source trustworthy?
  • When: Is the information current?
  • Where: Are sources cited?
  • Why: Was it meant to inform or mislead?
